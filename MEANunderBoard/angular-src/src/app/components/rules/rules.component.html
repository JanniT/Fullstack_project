Rules of UnderBoard

Prohibited Content

  • We do not allow content that promotes illegal activities.
  • Violent or graphic material is not allowed.
  • Content that incites violence, glorifies it, or organizes it is prohibited.
  • Additionally, this rule includes:
    • Any sexual material involving children, including drawings and fictional children.
    • Promotion or support of pedophilia (children under 16 years old) is prohibited.

Spamming

  • Excessive repetition or duplicate content is not allowed.

Harmful Content

  • Content containing harmful material or links to it is prohibited.
  • Link shorteners and other link hiders are not allowed.
  • Promotion of harassment of any website or service is prohibited.
  • Harmful content includes:
    • Phishing.
    • Malware or similar unwanted programs.
    • Jump-scare videos ("screamers").

Advertising

  • Content that advertises any commercial or profit-seeking service or product is not allowed.
  • Referrer links are allowed only if the person clicking the link benefits from it.
  • Advertising a competitor of Underboard or promoting one is prohibited.

Harassment

  • Content intended to harass or threaten others or promoting such actions is not allowed.
  • Stalking or "doxxing" is prohibited.

Sexual Content

  • Except in the "Body and Sex" and "Handicrafts" boards, where the material must be demonstrably original, we do not allow content that:
    • Contains nudity or any form of pornography, except non-excitatory art or news-worthy material in a non-sexual context related to the topic.

Inappropriate Content

  • Except in the "Random," "Bilderberg Club Room," and "International" boards, content that is:
    • Inappropriate towards others.
    • Contains excessive profanity.
    • Contains spoilers without a [spoiler] tag.
    • Engages in disputes about opinions, except in threads specifically created for that purpose.
    • Multiple user accounts per person are not allowed. Creating them may result in automatic account locking.

Terms of Use

  • By submitting a file to Underboard, you grant it for free non-commercial distribution. Commercial copyrights remain with you.
  • You are responsible for ensuring that you own the necessary copyrights to present your material on Underboard and that your message complies with all applicable laws.
  • Copying Underboard's content to another website or application, as well as automated copying for any purpose, is strictly prohibited.
  • Using the content for commercial purposes is prohibited. Content can be freely used for academic or public research.

Age Restrictions

  • The "Random," "Handicrafts," and "Body and Sex" areas are age-restricted and prohibited for those under 18 or minors without parental consent.

Rule Changes

  • Rules may be added, removed, or modified without separate notice.

Notification Requirement

  • All legally required reporting of offenses will be reported to the authorities.

Acceptance of Terms

By using this website, you acknowledge and accept the rules and terms outlined above for Underboard. You accept these terms by using this site.
